# 03 — SFX (ElevenLabs SFX v2, `eleven_text_to_sound_v2`)

`derived from ecosystem canon; do not edit as source`

Grammar (8-part, `gizmo-audio-canon/witnesses/sfx-prompt-grammar.md`): character/construction ·
one action · source materials · perceived weight · secondary details · duration/envelope ·
listening perspective · exclusions. Delivery: WAV 48kHz/16-bit one-shots; loudness measured, not
claimed (A2). Locomotion is modular (A6): body loop + surface contact + state overlay — never
one baked walk sound. Gizmo tonal center C#/E/G# (gizmo_clockwork motif). Pressure is diegetic
(A7): no alarms, no beeps, no countdown ticks anywhere.

**Global exclusions (append to every prompt):** *no cartoon squeak, no famous sci-fi droid
beeps, no giant industrial robot, no words or voices, no musical stinger.*
