# First-Level Procedural Layout

Date: 2026-06-21
Scope: OMX G005 — larger first playable arena using the existing world-kit pipeline.

## What changed

`godot/scenes/main.tscn` is no longer a compact 21-tile board. It is generated by
`tools/godot/generate_first_level_layout.py` from a deterministic coordinate plan:

- 91 walkable `clockwork_floor_tile_01` instances.
- 14 `clockwork_edge_pylon_01` perimeter/landmark instances.
- 1 imported `clockwork_north_beacon_01` major destination landmark on the north dais.
- 1 imported `clockwork_island_base_01` foundation under the arena.
- 12 imported debris fragments from `clockwork_debris_01/02` around the edge.
- 5 named level-zone markers under `ArenaTiles/LevelZones`:
  - `SouthLandingZone` — calmer spawn/entry runway.
  - `CentralGearPlazaZone` — main combat/readability space.
  - `NorthBeaconDaisZone` — first destination/hook landmark.
  - `WestScrapAlcoveZone` — side pocket for future pickup/enemy pressure.
  - `EastGearAlcoveZone` — side pocket for future pickup/enemy pressure.

The layout deliberately leaves negative space between the central plaza, diagonal
pads, side alcoves, and north/south dais areas so the stage reads as a floating
observatory rather than a filled rectangle.

## Pipeline

1. Blender pipeline (`tools/blender/gen_floating_islands.py`) creates the island
   base and debris GLBs in `godot/assets/world_kits/clockwork_observatory/`.
2. Godot wrapper scenes in `godot/scenes/world_kits/clockwork_observatory/` add
   world-kit metadata, collision, and camera-readable material styling.
3. `tools/godot/generate_first_level_layout.py` places those wrappers in
   `main.tscn` as a static, testable Godot scene.
4. `run_world_kit_tests.gd` verifies the wrappers; `run_playable_slice_tests.gd`
   verifies expanded arena scale, zone markers, debris, and landmark counts.

Run the layout generator from repo root after changing the coordinate plan:

```sh
python3 tools/godot/generate_first_level_layout.py
```

## Current contract

The first playable level should stay materially larger than the old prototype:
minimum 90 walkable floor tiles, exactly one imported major destination landmark,
at least 14 edge landmarks, at least 12 debris pieces, east-west span of at least
24 m, and north-south span of at least 28 m.
